TRAINYARD DIORAMA

Roles

3D Modeller and Texture Artist (Solo)

Project Summary

For an art project I created a trainyard scene from scratch. I started with some sketches and research into a layout for the scene and began greyboxing to get an idea of what models would be needed and the scale of the project.


After greyboxing I created a list of essential assets and began creating 3D models and the materials to go with them. I stuck with low poly to ensure I completed the scene in time.


Finally I imported everything into Unreal Engine to create a scene and combine the 3D models and materials. I also added some lighting for both day and night scenes.

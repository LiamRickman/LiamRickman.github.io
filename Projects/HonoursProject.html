HONOURS PROJECT

Roles

Gameplay & Mechanic Designer (Solo)

Project Summary

For my Honours Project I investigated replayability or replay value in games. For this I created a game prototype for a first-person looter shooter made in Unreal Engine 5.0.


My aim for the project is to create a core loop and at least one complete mission concept. I am specifically focusing on gameplay and mechanic design, however I am covering other areas as required.


The game includes replayable mechanics such as a loadout system with goal oriented unlocks, procedural level generation, and personalised mission generation.

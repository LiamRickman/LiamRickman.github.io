COLOUR VISION

Roles

Lead Programmer and Level Designer (Team: 7)

Project Summary

ColourVision is a 2D isometric dungeon crawler featuring a lizard wizard who has stolen all the colour from the world leaving it in greyscale.


The players goal is to bring back colour to the world by defeating the lizard wizard and his many minions.



As lead programmer I was in charge of the majority of the games programming and implementing assets to create the final product in the game engine of choice (Unity).


I also helped out with level design work primarily in-engine.

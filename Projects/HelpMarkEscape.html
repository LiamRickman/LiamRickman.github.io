HELP MARK ESCAPE

Roles

Lead Designer and Gameplay Programmer (Team: 7)

Project Summary

Help Mark Escape is a virtual visual novel experience that aims to teach young people about potentially dangerous scenarios they may encounter in life. It teaches them how to identify red flags about people and how they can defend themselves from them.


My role as Lead Designer saw me design the majority of the mechanics and game design documents. I also worked on level design within Unreal Engine and helped out the programmers later in the project.

THE LOST ARK

Roles

Level Designer and Mechanic Designer (Solo)

Project Summary

The Lost Ark is a level design project inspired by Indiana Jones and the Raiders of the Lost Ark's temple scene. This project was completed in Unreal Engine where I prototyped different traps and obstacles for the player to avoid on the way through the temple.


I started out by designing a level as close to the film as possible, noting any traps I wanted to recreate. I then went on to prototype these traps using Unreal Engine's blueprint system before redesigning the level to better accommodate the trap designs. The level went through many iterations before the final design


In the final version, the level featured a rope swing, light traps, swinging logs, floor puzzles and a boulder that chases the player.

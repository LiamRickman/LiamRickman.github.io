About Me


Hi! I'm Liam, a game designer living in Scotland. I have recently graduated from Abertay University where I have built up my game design skills!


I have been passionate about games for many years and since starting to learn how to design and produce my own games I haven't looked back.


I particularly enjoy prototyping, programming, and mechanic design however I have explored many areas of game development including UI/UX design, level design, 3D modelling, QA testing, and production.

Experience

Game Design Intern: Content | Rockstar Games

June 2022 - August 2022

In summer of 2022, I took part in a 10 week internship at Rockstar Games.

I was part of the content design team where I worked on creating various pieces of content such as missions and game modes using Rockstar's inhouse tools.

I collaborated with other designers to iterate and improve both my own and other's content.
